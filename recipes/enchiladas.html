Enchilada Recipe

Home

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes to make. Many people may feel intimidated, but I assure you they are not as complicated as you think.

Ingredients

Salsa Verde

  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Chicken Broth
  • Green chile peppers
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Cumin

Enchiladas

  • Shredded cheese
  • Corn tortillas
  • Canola oil
  • Shredded chicken

Directions

  1. Make salsa verde first: melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and saute til soft, about 5 minutes. Add flour and stir til fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Pour in broth, add green chiles, garlic, salt, and cumin. Simmer until flavors are blended, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Assemble enchiladas: mix in shredded cheese blends in a bowl
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry tortillas one a time, 10 to 20 seconds per side. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Dip tortillas into the salsa verde, then place them on a plate. Place shredded chicken and cheese down the center of the tortilla and roll it. Place seam side down into a shallow baking dish.
  6. Spoon salsa verde over enchiladas and sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
  7. Bake, uncovered, in the oven until sauce is bubbly and cheese is melted, about 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and garnish with cilantro(optional). Serve immediately and enjoy.
