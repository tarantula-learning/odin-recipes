Enchilada Recipe

Home

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes to make. Many people may feel intimidated, but I assure you they are not as complicated as you think.

Ingredients

Salsa Verde

  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Chicken Broth
  • Green chile peppers
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Cumin

Enchiladas

  • Shredded cheese
  • Corn tortillas
  • Canola oil
  • Shredded chicken

Directions

  1. Make salsa verde first: melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and saute til soft, about 5 minutes. Add flour and stire til fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Pour in broth, add green chiles, garlic, salt, and cumin. Simmer until flavors are blended, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat.
