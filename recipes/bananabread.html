Simple Banana Bread

Home

Description

You can ask anyone and they will tell you I am not a fan of baking. However, with this recipe, you can't go wrong...unless of course you forget the bananas.

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas
  • Box of yellow cake mix
  • Eggs
  • Mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Smash bananas in a large bowl, then add remaining ingredients and mix well
  3. Spray 2 small loaf pans or 1 large one with non stick cooking spray.
  4. Fill pans/pan with batter and bake for 35-40 minutes.
